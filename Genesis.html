Genesis
29 Oct 2016

                        In the beginning

                               God created

                                   the heavens and the earth

                        There was light

                              and there was darkness

                                  the first day

                        There was sky

                              and there was separated water

                                  the second day

                        There was land and seas

                              and there was vegetation and fruit

                                  the third day

                        There was sun and moon

                              and there were stars

                                  the fourth day

                        There were water creatures

                              and there were winged birds

                                  the fifth day

                        There were wild animals

                              and there was man

                                  the sixth day

                        The seventh day......

                                  is a new day......
